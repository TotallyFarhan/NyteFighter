Help

Objective

Your objective is to shoot the enemies and last as long as possible.

Rules

  • You have 10 lives. If an enemies goes past you and reaches the left side of the screen, you lose a life. Lose all your lives and the game is over
  • You immediately lose if an enemy makes contact with you
  • Shooting enemies awards you a certain amount of points. Visit the 'enemies section' to learn more.

Controls

  • Press the 'W' key to move up
  • Press the 'S' key to move down
  • There is no horizontal movement in this game
  • Click the mouse to shoot bullets
  • You start out with 50 bullets. Press the 'R' key to reload when necessary, or whenever you want to.

Meet the Enemies

The Regular Enemy

The regular enemy is the green medium sized enemy. It moves at a good speed, and takes 3 bullets to kill. It rewards 1 point when killed. It will always spawn throughout the game.

The Tank Enemy

The tank enemy is the pink huge sized enemy. It moves at a slow speed, and takes 10 bullets to kill. It rewards 5 points when killed. It will start spawning when you reach 50 points in score.

The Speedy Enemy

The speedy enemy is the yellow tiny enemy. It moves at a fast speed, and takes only 1 bullet to kill. It rewards 3 points when killed. It will start spawning when you reach 75 points in score.

PowerUps

As of now, there are six PowerUps in this game. There are more to come soon!

The Lives PowerUp

The Lives PowerUp is the blue circle with the heart. When you pick up this PowerUp, you get an extra life. It's useful when you're close to running out of lives and need something to help you get back up. At a time, you only get one life. Not as useful as the immunity, but still worth your time! It has a 33% chance of spawning.

The Immunity PowerUp

The Immunity PowerUp is the orange circle with the sheid. This PowerUp grants you immunity, meaning you cannot die from touching an enemy. Immunity is represented with a white border around the player. If an enemy touches you with the immunity shield, they immediately die and your immunity shield goes away, making you invulnerable again. It has a 16% chance of spawning.


The Speed PowerUp

The Speed PowerUp is the purple circle with the lightning bold. This PowerUp gives you a speed boost on your player, which lasts approximately 8 to 9 seconds. Pretty useful when trying to kill enemies that are far apart from each other, and you need to get to them quickly. It has a 10% chance of spawning.

The Wall PowerUp

The Wall PowerUp is the light-blue circle with the hammer. This PowerUp builds you a wall behind you, causing enemies to not be able to go behind you. This means that when an enemy goes behind you, instead of losing a life, it hits the wall and dies, which removes health from the wall. The wall stays up until it breaks, which takes 9 enemies to break. It has a 5% chance of spawning.

The Damage PowerUp

The Damage PowerUp is the red circle with the muscle. This PowerUp coats your bullets in a shining red armor, which makes them stronger and the enemies easier to kill. The regular enemy is now one-shot, the Tank enemy takes 5 shots, and the Speed enemy is also one-shot. The Boss Battle also is affected by the Damage PowerUp (subject to change). This PowerUp has a 20% chance of spawning.

The Slowness PowerUp

The Slowness PowerUp is the pink circle with the snowflake. This PowerUp causes the enemies to move at a slower pace, exactly half thier normal speed. This PowerUp lasts for about 5 seconds, which can allow you to calm down if enemies have been attacking you at a rapid rate for a while. This PowerUp is effective even during the Boss Battle, so the enemies that do spawn during the boss battle are slowed. This has a 14% chance of spawning.

The Ally PowerUp

The Ally PowerUp is the brown circle with the robot. This PowerUp allows for you to spawn in a helper robot called an ally to right the enemies with you. Each ally can take three hits from an enemy until it despawns. They shoot at a slow rate, and spawn in a random place on left side of the screen. You can only have two allies once, and once one dies, you have the chance for another one to spawn in. This has a 20% chance of spawning, every ten seconds while you have less than two allies. There will eventually be ally specific powerups, soon to come!

Boss Battle

When you reach 100 points, a Boss Battle begins!

The Boss Battle begins when you reach 100 or more points. It takes 130 bullets to kill the boss (changes based on Damage Multiplier). It has an outer shell that acts as a shield. During the boss battle, only the regular green enemies will spawn. You need to shoot the boss and the enemies at the same time. The boss's outer shell can take 100 hits before breaking. When the shield breaks, the boss is left vulnerable. This causes the regular green enemies to move faster, so they can defeat the player. It takes 30 hits after the shield breaks to kill the boss. After the boss is defeated, the game will resume like normal.
